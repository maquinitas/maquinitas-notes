# BOSS DR-880


## Installation

To control the Boss DR-880 with USB MIDI your Mac or Windows computer, install the driver from the "Downloads" section of the official [BOSS DR-880 website](https://www.boss.info/us/products/dr-880/).

You can also use the USB DIN jacks in the back panel.

## Power

The BOSS DR-880 can be powered with a BOSS AC power supply, BRC series. Its model number is BRC-230T for 230V AC input and outputs 14V AC, 0.8 A, 11.2 VA.

## USB MIDI instructions

## SETTING THE MIDI CHANNEL

* With playback stopped, press [EDIT].
* Move the cursor to the MIDI icon, and press [ENTER].
* Move the cursor to the "SETTING" icon and press [ENTER].
* Move the cursor to the "MIDI Ch. Drum" or "MIDI Ch. Bass" and use the VALUE dial to specify the MIDI channel for each.

Drum and bass should use different MIDI channels.
Program change messages are received on the MIDI channel of the drum part.
